What We Raise

Our Breeds

Selected for meat quality, temperament, and hardiness in our highland climate.

New Zealand White

Fast-growing meat breed with a calm temperament and excellent feed conversion. Our main line for meat production.

Californian

Hardy dual-purpose breed, known for good mothering and steady weight gain. Popular with new keepers too.

Chinchilla

Prized for its dense, soft coat and gentle nature — a favourite for pet buyers and 4-H style projects.

Palomino

A warm golden coat with a calm, easygoing temperament. Solid dual-purpose breed that does well in mixed housing.

Silver Marten

Sleek black or blue coat with silver-tipped guard hairs. Known for a lively personality and a striking look.

Angora

Grown for its long, wool-like fleece rather than meat. Needs regular grooming, and the fibre itself can be sold.

Mixed Breed

Rabbits with more than one breed in their line. Often just as healthy and hardy — a great affordable option for pet buyers.
